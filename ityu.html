IT YOUTH UNION

IT Youth Union is the organization that organizes many useful playgrounds for students of the IT Faculty in particular and the International University in general to exchange and connect. With the goal of "growing stronger and stronger", the IT Youth Union has been making constant efforts to create various interesting programs in many fields: volunteering, sports, academics, exchange and chat,...

IT HACKATHON: SOLANA CONSUMER HACK 16

Not just a simple competition, IT HACKATHON 2024 is an open playground for those who are passionate about technology, want to test themselves to be able to break through and build unique technology solutions. In 36 exciting and stressful hours, you will be able to unleash your creativity and cooperate with your teammates to turn unique ideas into reality, promising not to disappoint you.

WELCOME FRESHMEN 2024

To start off solidly and create momentum for the upcoming journey, ITYU will bring to the new students of the Faculty of Information Technology an extremely grand welcoming ceremony called Welcome Freshmen 2024. This is an annual meeting event for new IT students with teachers, seniors and friends in the same faculty.
